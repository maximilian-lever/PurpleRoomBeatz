About Dennis

My background in music started at a young age working in various venues, most being nightclubs and large studio settings, other contributory factors to my knowledge of sound and acoustics is based on working closely with professional recording artists, this gives me insight to what is being looked for and how I can improve on what is already being used.

I have built studios for a number of radio stations and this developed my curiosity in acoustics further, building radio stations developed my knowledge of room isolation. This led to me creating my own design of studio insulation for clients that didn’t want to be heard. Having built these studios for over two decades, it has now become my expertise and I now design and build a variety of studios for this use.

My formal qualifications include Basic Music Theory, HND & HNC in Music Theory, Sound Design and Foley, I then decided to challenge myself further and complete a BA Honours in Sound Engineering. By completing these formal qualification’s, I gained an intense theoretical understanding of sound design in relation to acoustics.
